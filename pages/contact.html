FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are some common questions we receive.

How long does a project take?
Project timelines vary based on complexity. A typical website takes 2-4 weeks, while complex platforms like VTU or School Management systems take 4-8 weeks.
What's your payment structure?
We require 50% deposit to start, 25% on progress review, and 25% upon completion. All payments in Naira (₦) via bank transfer.
Do you provide post-launch support?
Yes! All projects include 30 days free support. We also offer affordable maintenance packages for ongoing support.
Are your websites mobile-friendly?
Absolutely! All our websites are fully responsive and optimized for all devices - mobile, tablet, and desktop.
Can you help with SEO?
Yes! We include basic SEO optimization in all websites. Advanced SEO packages are available for businesses wanting to rank higher.
Can I update the website myself?
Yes! We provide CMS solutions (WordPress, custom admin panels) that allow you to easily update content without technical knowledge.
